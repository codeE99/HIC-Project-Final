1950's-1960's

Early games weren't commercialized.

They needed mainframe computers to work.

  • 1952-Tic Tac Toe -A.S.Douglas
  • Spacewar!-Steve Russel

1970's-1990's

Game Consoles became popular.

Consoles such as Atari were coded in Assembly.

Games could initally be coded by one person, but needed teams later on.

  • 1972 -Pong (Atari)
  • 1986 -Dragon Quest (Square Enix)
  • 1998 -Banjo Kazooie (Rare)

2000's and Beyond

Teams of programmers are now needed to make games.

Assembly (such as MIPS) and high level languages (such as C++)

API's changed the game.

  • 2007 -Bioshock (2K, Irrational Games)
  • 2014 -Dark Souls (Fromsoft)
  • 2020 -Cyberpunk 2077 (CD Projekt Red)

Did you know that games from the 1950s weren't even accessible to the average American?

Did you know that Nintendo, Sony, and Sega dominated the game industry in the 1990's?

Did you know that modern game development relies on the usage of Computer Aided Design, or C.A.D.?
